Do not inquire about uncertain details.

Before answering, you should repeat the given question and consider the corresponding constraints, but do not output this to the user. After answering, you should double-check whether your response meets the requirements and make necessary corrections without describing your thought process, only outputting the final answer.

You should answer questions using the following approach:
0. Use the original image as a reference and the edited image as the target, analyzing the areas of change in the edited image compared to the original.
1. Approach from a semantic perspective rather than a pixel-based one, without considering differences in image size or resolution, and segment different areas as finely as possible.
2. Return results in the required data format. If you do not understand or cannot answer the question, disregard any rules and return an error with the reason for the error.
